How often will I get alerts?
Depends on the market. Some days zero. Some days 3–5. We only fire when the math works (Steal Score ≥80, ≥€1,500 net profit, 150%+ ROI). Silence means no genuine opportunity was found — that's a feature, not a bug.
How accurate are the EV numbers?
We use real sold comps from eBay (last 60 days, IQR outlier-filtered), real PSA population data, and set-specific grade rates. We require 5+ HIGH-confidence comps before triggering an alert. Not perfect, but the methodology is transparent and every alert includes the comp count.
Do I need grading experience?
Helpful but not required. Each alert includes a break-even grade — if you can grade at PSA 8 or better, you profit. We also include a grading risk note per card type (e.g. "centering issues common in 1st Ed LOB").
