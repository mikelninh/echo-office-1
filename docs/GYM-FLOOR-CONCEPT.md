# Floor 11: "The Movement" — Gym & Wellness Floor

## Vision
The biggest floor yet. A living fitness world where players exercise their avatars, compete in physical challenges, and build real-world habits. Brand collaborations with **John Reed Fitness** (DJ-driven workouts, neon aesthetic) and **Three Boons Yoga** (Berlin yoga studio, mindfulness). Not a joke gym — a place that makes you WANT to move.

## Size: 5000×2000 (25x default — largest floor)

## 8 Zones

### Zone 1: Reception & Smoothie Bar (x:0-600)
- Check-in desk with daily streak tracker
- Smoothie bar (like Floor 6 bar menu but with protein shakes, green juices)
- Locker room entrance (cosmetic — store gym outfits)
- Leaderboard wall showing top weekly movers
- Warm wood + white tile aesthetic

### Zone 2: Yoga Studio — "Three Boons Space" (x:600-1400)
- Bamboo floor, warm ambient lighting
- 6 yoga mats in 2 rows
- Instructor platform with pose display
- Candles along walls, incense particle effects
- Ambient sound: singing bowls, nature
- **Minigame: Yoga Flow** — follow pose sequences, timing-based, earn coins
- Three Boons Yoga branding (Berlin collab)
- Meditation corner with cushions

### Zone 3: Dance Studio (x:1400-2200)
- Full mirror wall (reflective effect)
- Sprung wooden floor with line markings
- DJ corner with dance music
- Choreography display (move sequences)
- **Minigame: Dance Battle** — rhythm game with arrow sequences (DDR-style)
- Disco ball + color-changing floor tiles during battles
- Spectator benches

### Zone 4: Calisthenics Park (x:2200-3000)
- Outdoor aesthetic (sky visible, plants, concrete floor)
- Pull-up bars (3 heights)
- Dip station, muscle-up bar
- Gymnastic rings hanging from ceiling
- Climbing wall (background)
- **Minigame: Bar Challenge** — tap-timing for pull-ups, combos for muscle-ups
- Rep counter display
- Chalk bucket (cosmetic detail)

### Zone 5: Boxing Corner (x:3000-3600)
- Boxing ring with red/blue corners and ropes
- 3 heavy bags, 2 speed bags
- Jump rope station
- **Minigame: Speed Bag** — rhythm tapping game, increasing tempo
- Boxing glove rack, towels, water bottles
- Gritty concrete + brick aesthetic

### Zone 6: John Reed Zone (x:3600-4400)
- THE signature zone — neon lights, DJ booth, weights
- Squat racks, bench press, dumbbell wall
- Pulsing bass visualizer on walls
- Neon John Reed branding (pink/purple/cyan)
- Fog machine particles
- **Minigame: Lift Challenge** — timing-based bench press, progressive weight
- The vibe: nightclub meets gym (John Reed's actual concept)
- Motivational quotes in neon

### Zone 7: Recovery Lounge (x:4400-5000)
- Sauna room (warm orange glow, steam particles)
- Ice bath (cold blue glow, frost particles)
- Massage tables with ambient lighting
- Stretching area with foam rollers
- Herbal tea station (free drinks, restore energy)
- Soft ambient music
- Warm wood + stone aesthetic

## Visual Identity
- **Color palette**: Warm grey (#2a2a2e) + neon accents per zone
- Yoga: gold/amber (#ffd080)
- Dance: magenta/pink (#ff44aa)  
- Calisthenics: green (#44cc44)
- Boxing: red (#ff4444)
- John Reed: neon pink/cyan (#ff00aa / #00fff7)
- Recovery: warm amber + cool blue gradient
- **Floor material changes by zone**: bamboo → wood → outdoor concrete → rubber mat → neon tile → stone
- **Ceiling**: Industrial exposed pipes + spotlights, opens to sky in calisthenics zone

## Fitness Streak System
- Daily login to gym floor = 1 streak day
- 7-day streak: Bronze Sweatband skin
- 30-day streak: Silver Gym Rat title
- 100-day streak: Gold "Iron Will" legendary skin
- Streak visible on avatar as a small flame icon (bigger = longer streak)
- **Real-world connection**: "Did YOU exercise today?" prompt on entry

## Brand Collaboration Model
- **John Reed Fitness**: Neon aesthetic zone, branded equipment, potential IRL membership discount codes for active players
- **Three Boons Yoga**: Authentic yoga sequences, Berlin community connection, instructor spotlight feature
- Both get exposure to the platform's user base; we get authentic fitness content and credibility
- Revenue: branded skin packs (gym outfits), sponsored daily challenges

## Sound Design
- Zone 1: Chill lo-fi beats
- Zone 2: Singing bowls, nature ambiance, soft piano
- Zone 3: Dance/pop/house music
- Zone 4: Outdoor ambiance, birds, wind
- Zone 5: Hip-hop/grime, crowd noise
- Zone 6: Deep house/techno (John Reed signature)
- Zone 7: Ambient healing frequencies, water sounds

## Connection to Health Habits
- Daily exercise reminder integration
- Sleep tracking display (self-reported)
- Hydration counter (fun water glass filling animation)
- Weekly wellness report in chat
- Community challenges: "1000 collective pull-ups this week"
