## Revenue Streams — How This Pays for Itself

### 1. 🔗 Affiliate Revenue (Immediate — Week 1)
**How:** Every card link in the dashboard earns a commission when someone buys.
- **eBay Partner Network**: 1-4% commission on sales. Free to join.
- **TCGPlayer Affiliate Program**: 5% commission. Mass market.
- **Cardmarket Affiliate** (EU): Apply for partnership. Dominant in EU.
- **Amazon Associates**: For sealed product links. 1-3%.

**Revenue estimate:** Even modest traffic (500 visitors/month) at 2% conversion = €50-200/month from affiliate links alone. At scale (5K visitors) = €500-2000/month.

**Action:** Replace all eBay/TCGPlayer links with affiliate links. Add "Buy Now" buttons to card alerts.

### 2. 💎 Premium Dashboard (Month 2-3)
**Free tier:** What we have now — market overview, basic alerts, 24h history.
**Premium tier ($5-10/month):**
- Real-time alerts (push notifications when a holy grail drops)
- Price history graphs (30/90/365 day)
- Custom watchlists (track specific cards)
- Price predictions (trend analysis)
- SMS/Telegram/Discord alerts
- Portfolio tracker (what you own, current value, P&L)
- Arbitrage finder (same card cheaper on another platform)

**Revenue estimate:** 100 premium users × $7/month = $700/month. 1000 users = $7K/month.

### 3. 🎰 Gacha / Collection Game (Month 3-4)
**This is the NIKKE / Genshin insight.** Don't just track cards — gamify the experience.

**How it works in Echo's Vault (Floor 13):**
- **Daily login**: Roll a free gacha once per day — win a digital card for your virtual shelf
- **Digital card collection**: Pixel-art versions of real cards. Cosmetic, but collectible.
- **Rarity tiers**: Common → Rare → Super Rare → Ultra Rare → Secret Rare → Ghost Rare
- **Pity system**: Guaranteed SR at 50 rolls, UR at 100 (NIKKE-style)
- **Card leveling**: Duplicate digital cards = XP to level up your favorites
- **Display cases**: Show off your digital collection in your Vault room
- **Seasonal events**: Limited-edition digital cards tied to real TCG releases

**The bridge between digital and physical:**
- Scan a real card (QR/barcode) → unlock its digital twin in the Vault
- Physical card verified → special holo border on digital version
- Trade digital cards with other Vault users
- **"Cardfolio"**: Photo your real collection, AI identifies cards, builds digital portfolio

**Revenue:**
- Premium gacha rolls: $1 for 10 rolls (cosmetic only, never pay-to-win)
- Special holo/animated card frames: $0.50-2 each
- Seasonal pass: $5/month for exclusive digital cards + premium dashboard
- **This is the NIKKE model**: the pull mechanic is the business. Keep it fair, keep it fun.

### 4. 📊 Data & Intelligence (Month 4-6)
**You're sitting on market data nobody else has.** Cross-TCG, multi-platform, historical.
- **Monthly market report** (free, builds audience) — published blog/social
- **API access** ($20-50/month) — for other developers building TCG tools
- **Price guide integration** — partner with existing TCG communities
- **Market alerts for shops/dealers** ($30-100/month) — B2B pricing intelligence

### 5. 🏪 Community Marketplace (Month 6+)
**Not competing with eBay.** Instead: curated, verified, collector-to-collector.
- List verified cards with your Cardfolio photos
- Escrow + authentication service (partner with PSA/CGC)
- 3-5% transaction fee
- Trust scores based on Vault activity

## What Makes This Different

1. **Cross-TCG**: Nobody tracks 10 TCGs in one place. Everyone is siloed.
2. **Holy Grail focus**: We track the €5K+ market. This is where serious money moves.
3. **Digital-physical bridge**: Not "NFTs" (dead word). Real utility: scan your card, track your collection, see its value change.
4. **Built inside a game**: The Vault isn't a boring spreadsheet. It's a room you walk into, with display cases and a gacha machine. Nobody else has this.
5. **Collector-first**: Built by a collector, for collectors. Not by a corporation.
