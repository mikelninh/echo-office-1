Based on everything you've done — your tastes, your habits, your collecting style — your companion crystallizes into their full form. A character that is UNIQUELY yours. Not randomly generated garbage. A coherent design that feels like it was drawn by an artist who knows you.

## Your Partner On Missions

### Mission Types

**🎴 Holy Grail Hunts**
Your companion scouts the market with you.
- "I found something! Look at this Blue-Eyes listing!" (price alerts become companion dialogue)
- They react to deals: excited for underpriced grails, suspicious of overpriced ones
- They remember your collection goals: "You said you wanted a PSA 10 Dandadan. I'm watching."
- When you land a grail: full celebration animation. They're proud of you.
- When you miss one: "Next time. I'll keep looking."

**🎰 Gacha Pulls**
Your companion pulls WITH you.
- They stand next to the gacha machine
- On the pull animation: they're watching, nervous, hopeful
- Bad pull: they comfort you. "That's OK, we're at 47 pity now. Three more."
- Good pull: they GO CRAZY. Dancing, sparkles, hugging the new card.
- Mythic pull: they FAINT from excitement. Then get up and celebrate.
- The companion transforms gacha from gambling into a shared experience.

**📊 Market Analysis**
Your companion comments on the dashboard.
- "Lorcana is up 4.8% today. Should we look?"
- "Yu-Gi-Oh has been stable all week. Quiet market."
- "Three new alerts overnight! Let me show you."
- They're not an AI chatbot — they speak in character, with personality, in short bursts.

**⚔️ Battles & Competitions**
Your companion fights alongside you.
- Tournament mode: your companion + your card collection = your battle power
- They have abilities based on their evolution path
- They level up through battles, gaining new moves and visual upgrades
- Ranked ladder: your companion's power is a social flex

**🌍 Exploration**
Your companion walks the station with you.
- They react to each floor differently
- They find hidden items, secrets, easter eggs
- They interact with OTHER users' companions
- They have favorite spots (based on where you spend time)

## The Relationship Arc

This is what makes it MORE than a pet and MORE than a collectible.

### Stage 1: Stranger (Day 1-3)
Your egg hatches. Your companion is curious but cautious. Short responses. Watches you from a distance. Learning who you are.

### Stage 2: Acquaintance (Week 1-2)  
They start reacting to your activity. Small comments. Follows you between floors. Shows interest in what you collect. Still figuring out their personality.

### Stage 3: Friend (Month 1)
Full personality emerged. They have opinions. "I like this card more than that one." They greet you by name when you log in. They remember your habits. First real bond.

### Stage 4: Partner (Month 2-3)
They actively help on missions. Market alerts feel like tips from a friend. Gacha pulls are shared excitement. They protect your collection ("Someone offered 200 coins for that card. I think it's worth more."). Inside jokes develop based on shared history.

### Stage 5: Soulbound (Month 6+)
They know you better than any algorithm. Their evolution reflects your entire journey. Your collection and their form are intertwined. They've been through wins and losses with you. Seeing them IS seeing your own story. 

The emotional weight: **you can't get this companion anywhere else.** No one can buy it. No one can copy it. It was born from YOUR journey. It's the most valuable thing on your account — and it's not tradeable.
