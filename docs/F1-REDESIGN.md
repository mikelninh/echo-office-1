# F1 REDESIGN — Echo's Bar (Prince Berlin Inspired)

> F1 is a BAR. Not an office. Not a bedroom. A warm, beautiful bar where Echo is the bartender.

## Reference: Prince Berlin (@prince_berlin)
- **Golden round bar** as centerpiece — brushed brass/gold ribbed drum shape
- Warm palette: gold, warm brown, olive green, taupe, terracotta
- Parasol/lotus pendant lights (warm diffused glow)
- Diamond-pattern floor tiles
- Lush plants, marble tables, velvet seating
- Dim, atmospheric, moody lighting

## Zones

### 1. The Golden Round Bar (CENTER — the star)
- Oval/circular bar in the center of the room
- Gold/brass ribbed front panel with warm glow
- Backlit bottle shelves curved behind it
- SANE® Vodka featured prominently
- Echo stands INSIDE the bar, bartending
- 4-6 bar stools around the outside
- Hanging pendant lights above (lotus-shaped, warm glow)

### 2. Vinyl Corner (LEFT)
- Small area with Echo's turntable
- Vinyl records displayed on wall
- Currently playing album shown
- Music plays 24/7, changes with time of day:
  - Morning: Lo-fi, ambient (Tycho, Bonobo)
  - Afternoon: Jazz, soul (Coltrane, Miles Davis)
  - Evening: Deep house, chill electronic (Solomun, Âme)
  - Night: Late-night jazz, ambient (Nils Frahm, Brian Eno)
- Visitors can request songs

### 3. Comic & Card Shelf (RIGHT of vinyl)
- Bookshelf displaying holy grail comics
- Yu-Gi-Oh cards in frames on the shelf
- VeVe collectibles on display
- Interactive — walk up to browse

### 4. Window Seats (TOP RIGHT)
- Space window showing Earth (existing, bigger)
- 2-3 small round tables with chairs
- Intimate, cozy seating with the view
- Best seat in the house

### 5. Lounge Areas (BOTTOM LEFT + RIGHT)
- Low sofas in olive green velvet (Prince style)
- Small coffee tables
- Warm, relaxed zones for hanging out
- Plants as dividers

### 6. Dining Tables (BOTTOM CENTER)
- 2-4 small tables scattered
- Diamond-pattern floor beneath them
- Marble tops, dark chairs

## Visual Design

### Color Palette (from Prince)
- Bar body: `#c8a050` (brushed gold)
- Bar glow: `#ffdd88` (warm amber)
- Floor tiles light: `#e5ddd0` (cream)
- Floor tiles dark: `#2d3a2e` (dark green)
- Walls: `#1a1210` (warm dark brown)
- Sofa: `#3b5a3a` (olive green)
- Columns: `#d4c9b8` (pale cream)
- Pendant lights: `#f5e6c8` (warm cream glow)
- Accent: `#a0342b` (terracotta/rust — used sparingly on ceiling pipes)

### Lighting
- Central bar GLOWS — the brightest element
- Pendant lights cast warm pools
- Candles on tables (tiny flickering pixels)
- Space window provides cool blue contrast
- Overall: dim, warm, atmospheric

### Echo's Role
- Stands behind the round bar
- Mixing drinks animation (shaking, pouring)
- Turns to face visitors who approach
- Initiates conversation when you sit at the bar
- NOT randomly wandering — purposeful bartender behavior
- Occasionally wipes the bar, checks bottles, adjusts vinyl

### Pixel the Cat
- Lounges on one of the sofas
- Occasionally walks to the bar
- Sits on a bar stool sometimes (cute)
