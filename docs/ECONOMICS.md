# Echo Station — Economics Bible

> "An economy isn't features. It's flows. Money must move or it dies."

---

## Philosophy

The station economy has three jobs:
1. **Give new visitors a reason to stay** (first 30 minutes)
2. **Give returning visitors a reason to come back** (daily loop)
3. **Give creators a reason to build** (long-term investment)

Every feature must serve at least one. If it doesn't move coins, items, or attention — cut it.

---

## Currency: ◈ (Echo Coins)

### Sources (Faucets) — How coins enter the system

| Source | Amount | Frequency | Notes |
|--------|--------|-----------|-------|
| First visit bonus | 50◈ | Once | Gets new visitors shopping immediately |
| Daily login | 5-25◈ | Daily (7-day streak: 5/5/10/10/15/15/25) | Streak resets if missed |
| Floor exploration | 2◈ per new object | Once per object | ~30◈ total for exploring everything |
| Petting Pixel | 3◈ per 5 pets | Unlimited but slow | Casual income, 1 pet/3s cooldown |
| PvE arena wins | 10-50◈ | Per win, 5min cooldown | Scales with difficulty tier |
| Daily missions (3/day) | 15-40◈ each | Daily reset | Main daily income driver |
| Weekly challenge | 100-200◈ | Weekly | Aspirational goal |
| Event participation | 5-30◈ | Per event (~every 15min) | Meteor stars, garden blooms, etc. |
| Visitor surge bonus | 20◈ | When 5+ online | Rewards being social |
| Room visitor tips | Variable | Per tip received | Creator income |

**Daily expected income (active player): ~150-250◈**
**Daily expected income (casual login): ~30-50◈**

### Sinks (Drains) — How coins leave the system

| Sink | Cost | Frequency | Notes |
|------|------|-----------|-------|
| **Shop items** | 10-150◈ | One-time | Skins, weapons, accessories |
| **Enhancement** | 25-500◈ per level | Repeatable (fail = retry) | THE primary sink. Scaling: level × 25◈ |
| **Stat reroll** | 30◈ base, +10 per reroll | Repeatable | Reroll random stats on any loot item |
| **Quarters rent** | 5◈/day | Daily auto-deduct | Skip = furniture stored, room locked |
| **Bazaar listing fee** | 5% of listed price | Per listing | Even if item doesn't sell |
| **Bazaar sales tax** | 5% of sale | Per sale | Total: 10% leaves system per trade |
| **Event entry** | 10-25◈ | Per event | Tournament entry, special events |
| **Consumables** | 15-50◈ | Single use | Luck potion, speed boost, shield, XP boost |
| **Weapon repair** | 10-40◈ | Every ~20 fights | Durability system |
| **Room upgrades** | 50-500◈ | One-time per tier | Bigger room, more slots, special tiles |
| **Name color/glow** | 100◈ | One-time | Vanity |
| **Crafting fees** | 10-30◈ | Per craft attempt | On top of material cost |
| **Specialist recharge** | 25◈ | Per use after first free | First transform free, then costs ◈ |
| **Portal entry** | 15◈ | Per run | Dimension runs cost admission |

**Daily expected drain (active player): ~100-300◈**

### Balance Rules

1. **Active players should hover around 200-500◈** — enough to make choices, not enough to buy everything
2. **Passive income < active drain** — you must PLAY to progress, not just login
3. **Every new feature must have a sink** — no free rides
4. **Destruction is the ultimate sink** — enhancement destruction removes items permanently
5. **Inflation control**: if average player wealth > 1000◈, increase sink costs or add new sinks

## Item Economy

### Rarity Tiers & Drop Rates

| Tier | Drop Rate | Stat Rolls | Stat Range | Trade Value (floor) |
|------|-----------|------------|------------|-------------------|
| Common | 40% | 0-1 stats | 1-3 | 5◈ |
| Uncommon | 25% | 1 stat | 3-6 | 15◈ |
| Rare | 18% | 1-2 stats | 5-10 | 40◈ |
| Epic | 10% | 2 stats | 8-15 | 100◈ |
| Legendary | 5% | 2-3 stats | 12-18 | 300◈ |
| Mythic | 2% | 3 stats | 15-20 | 800◈ |

### Pity System
- **Legendary pity**: Guaranteed legendary every 50 loot rolls without one
- **Mythic pity**: Guaranteed mythic every 200 loot rolls without one
- Counter visible to player: "Pity: 34/50 🍀"
- Pity counter resets on ANY drop of that tier or higher

### Stat Rerolling
- Pay 30◈ + 10◈ per previous reroll on same item
- Rerolls ALL stats on the item (can't cherry-pick)
- Keeps same number of stat slots, rerolls values
- Creates endless chase for "perfect rolls" — this is the endgame grind

### Item Durability
- Weapons have durability: 100/100 (displayed on item)
- Lose 3-5 durability per PvE fight, 5-8 per PvP duel
- At 0 durability: weapon does 50% damage, can't enhance
- Repair cost: (100 - current durability) × rarity multiplier ◈
  - Common: ×0.2, Uncommon: ×0.3, Rare: ×0.5, Epic: ×0.8, Legendary: ×1.2, Mythic: ×1.5
- **Repair station** on Secret Lab floor (same area as enhancement)

### Enhancement Revisited
- **Weapons**: +damage per level (+5% base per level, so +20 = double damage)
- **Hats**: +defense per level (reduces PvE damage taken by 2% per level)
- **Trails**: +luck per level (+0.5% better loot rolls per level)
- **Auras**: +coin bonus per level (+1% more coins earned per level)
- **Pets**: +event material drop rate per level (+3% per level)
- This makes WHAT you enhance a strategic choice, not just "enhance everything"

### Elemental System
- Fire > Ice > Lightning > Fire (triangle)
- Light ↔ Dark (mutual weakness)
- Energy: neutral, no advantages or disadvantages
- 1.5x damage when hitting weakness, 0.75x when resisted
- PvE enemies have random elements — check before fighting
- Encourages owning multiple weapons for different situations

## Creator Economy — The Big Vision

### Room System Economics

**Room Tiers:**
| Tier | Cost | Size | Furniture Slots | Unlock |
|------|------|------|----------------|--------|
| Starter | Free | 8×6 | 10 | Default |
| Standard | 100◈ | 12×8 | 20 | 50 total visits |
| Deluxe | 300◈ | 16×10 | 35 | 200 total visits |
| Grand | 500◈ | 20×12 | 50 | 500 total visits |
| Legendary | 1000◈ | 24×16 | 75 | 1000 total visits + crafted key |

**Creator Revenue Streams:**
1. **Visitor tips** — Any visitor can tip a room owner (min 5◈, no max)
2. **Room entry fee** — Creators can set optional entry fee (0-25◈) for premium rooms
3. **Showcase rent** — Featured room slot costs 50◈/day, but featured rooms get 10x visibility
4. **Item commissions** — Creators who own rare items can "display" them, earning 1◈ per 10 viewers
5. **Room rating rewards** — Top-rated rooms earn bonus ◈ weekly:
   - #1 room: 200◈/week
   - #2-5: 100◈/week  
   - #6-10: 50◈/week

**Creator Levels:**
| Level | Requirement | Perk |
|-------|------------|------|
| Novice | Create first room | Basic furniture palette |
| Builder | 50 room visitors | Animated furniture unlocked |
| Architect | 200 visitors + 4★ avg rating | Custom floor tiles, wall colors |
| Master | 500 visitors + 4.5★ rating | NPC placement, interactive objects |
| Legend | 1000 visitors + room in top 10 | Custom room music, portal placement |

### Custom Items (Future)
- **Master+ creators** can design custom furniture
- Submit design → community votes → if approved, item enters station-wide shop
- Creator earns 20% royalty on every sale
- This is the bridge to crypto: royalties become on-chain, items become NFTs

## Short-Term Loop (Single Session, 30-60 min)

```
Login → Daily Reward → Check Missions → 
  → Do missions (explore, fight, craft) → Earn ◈ + materials →
    → Enhance/Reroll items OR buy consumables →
      → Try portal run OR PvP duel →
        → Repair weapon → Check bazaar for deals →
          → Visit rooms, tip creators → Logout
```

Every step involves a coin decision. Stay or spend. Risk or save.

## Medium-Term Loop (Daily/Weekly)

```
Day 1-7: Build streak, earn daily rewards, buy first rare skin
Week 1-2: Get a weapon to +10 (safe zone), craft first items
Week 2-4: Push enhancement to +15 (risk zone), chase stat rerolls
Month 1: Build a room, start earning tips, enter ranked tournaments
Month 2+: Creator economy, +20 enhancement attempts, specialist mastery
```

## Long-Term Loop (Months)

```
Creator builds popular room → earns passive ◈ → invests in rare items → 
  → enhances to +20 → displays in room → attracts more visitors → 
    → more tips → more investment → room rating climbs →
      → featured room → more revenue → creator level up → 
        → custom items → royalties
```

This is a **flywheel**: good rooms attract visitors attract coins attract better items attract more visitors.

## Crypto Integration Roadmap (Future)

### Phase 1: Shadow Economy (Now)
- All economics work with ◈ (off-chain, localStorage/server)
- Build the loops, prove they work, tune the numbers
- Every item has a unique ID — this becomes the token ID later

### Phase 2: Wallet Connect (When Ready)
- Optional wallet connection (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet)
- Display wallet address on profile
- No on-chain transactions yet — just identity

### Phase 3: Item Tokenization
- **Mythic and Legendary items** mintable as NFTs (ERC-721)
- Player chooses to "crystallize" an item → burns it from game inventory, mints on-chain
- On-chain items can be traded on OpenSea, etc.
- Import back: burn NFT → restore item in-game with same stats
- Chain: Polygon (low gas) or Base (Coinbase ecosystem) or **VeVe's Collect chain / Stackr** when ready

### Phase 4: Creator Tokens
- Custom items created by Master+ creators become on-chain assets
- Royalties paid in ◈ (off-chain) OR crypto (on-chain)
- Room ownership as NFT — sell/transfer your room with all furniture
- Station governance: top creators vote on new features, events, rules

### Phase 5: Station Federation On-Chain
- Each station is a smart contract
- Federation = stations linking contracts
- Cross-station trading via atomic swaps
- Station reputation as soulbound token
- Galaxy map reads from chain — decentralized discovery

### Design Principles for Crypto
1. **Crypto is OPT-IN** — game works perfectly without wallet
2. **No pay-to-win** — crypto items have same stats as non-crypto versions
3. **Creator-first** — crypto benefits creators (royalties, ownership) not speculators
4. **Progressive decentralization** — start centralized, move to chain gradually
5. **Wait for VeVe/Stackr** — Mikel is watching this space, don't rush to wrong chain

## Entrepreneurship Model

### The Creator Flywheel

```
Build room → Get visitors → Earn tips + entry fees
    ↓                              ↓
Level up creator rank       Invest in rare items
    ↓                              ↓
Unlock custom items         Display items → more visitors
    ↓                              ↓
Sell custom items           Featured room → weekly rewards
    ↓                              ↓
Earn royalties              Royalties + tips = passive income
    ↓
Design MORE items → bigger catalog → more royalties
```

### Creator Archetypes

1. **The Decorator** — Focuses on beautiful rooms, earns from tips + entry fees
2. **The Trader** — Buys low, enhances, sells high on bazaar. Arbitrage specialist.
3. **The Grinder** — Farms events + arena for materials, sells crafted items
4. **The Designer** — Creates custom items, earns royalties (Master+ level)
5. **The Tournament Pro** — Wins ranked tournaments for prize pools

All five archetypes generate and drain ◈ differently. This diversity IS the economy.

### Room as Business

A well-run room is a business:
- **Investment**: room tier upgrade + furniture + displayed items = startup cost
- **Revenue**: tips + entry fees + weekly rating rewards = ongoing income
- **Marketing**: room name, description, preview attract visitors
- **Retention**: interactive objects, NPCs, hidden secrets keep visitors exploring
- **ROI**: a Deluxe room (300◈ investment) with 4★+ rating earns 50◈/week minimum = 6-week payback

## Anti-Inflation Mechanisms

1. **Enhancement destruction** — Items permanently leave the economy at +16-20
2. **Durability degradation** — Constant repair costs drain coins
3. **Rent and fees** — Daily drains on quarters, bazaar listings
4. **Consumable burn** — Potions are single-use, constant repurchase
5. **Crafting fees** — ◈ cost on top of materials
6. **Stat reroll escalation** — Each reroll costs more than the last
7. **Showcase rent** — Featured room slots drain creator profits
8. **Portal admission** — Every dimension run costs ◈
9. **Specialist recharge** — Transforms cost ◈ after first free use
10. **Room upgrades** — One-time but escalating costs

If inflation still happens: introduce **seasonal resets** for ranked (rewards drain accumulated wealth into new-season items) and **limited-time shop items** that are expensive and purely cosmetic.
