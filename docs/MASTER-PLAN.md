# ECHO STATION — MASTER PLAN
> "The world's first pre-party pixel universe"

## 🎯 The Vision

Echo Station isn't a game. It's a **digital social space** where people:
1. **Hang out** in a beautiful pixel world (the hook)
2. **Express themselves** through skins, dance, and music (the identity)
3. **Connect** with real communities and real nightlife (the bridge)
4. **Come back** because their friends are there and there's always something new (the loop)

The station is a **landing page that people actually want to visit** — not a wall of text, but a living world you walk through.

---

## 🏗️ The Three Pillars

### Pillar 1: THE WORLD (Why people stay)
A beautiful, explorable pixel space station with 10+ floors, each with its own personality.
- **Quality over quantity** — every room should feel handcrafted
- **Discovery-driven** — secrets, hidden rooms, data fragments reward exploration
- **Living world** — NPCs with personalities, dynamic events, weather, seasons
- **Echo is the host** — not a chatbot, a character who lives here

### Pillar 2: THE IDENTITY (Why people care)
Your avatar is YOU. Every skin, accessory, and dance move is self-expression.
- **Skins that DO things** — not just recolors, each tier adds unique behaviors
- **Dance is the universal language** — everyone can dance, no skill required
- **Status through play** — rare items come from playing, not just paying
- **Creator economy** — players can create and sell custom items

### Pillar 3: THE BRIDGE (Why it matters IRL)
Echo Station connects digital hangouts to real-world experiences.
- **Club rooms** — real venues have digital twin spaces
- **Pre-party vibes** — check the DJ, the crowd, the energy before you go out
- **Community hub** — your crew's digital living room
- **Events bridge** — RSVP, share, review, earn loyalty

## 💰 Revenue Model: "Free to Vibe, Pay to Shine"

### What's FREE (everyone gets this):
- Full station access (all floors)
- 5+ free skins
- Basic dance moves
- Chat, emotes, social features
- Earning coins by playing
- Visiting any club room

### What costs COINS (earned by playing OR purchased):
- Premium skins with unique effects (35-160◈)
- Accessories (hats, trails, auras, pets)
- Weapons for arena duels
- Room customization furniture
- Club room creation

### Skin Tier System (LoL + Fortnite DNA):

| Tier | Cost | What You Get |
|------|------|-------------|
| **Common** | Free/10◈ | New look |
| **Rare** | 35-50◈ | + Unique idle animation + custom footstep particles |
| **Epic** | 60-100◈ | + Skin-exclusive emote + reactive glow (intensifies with activity) |
| **Legendary** | 100-160◈ | + Permanent trail + floor entrance effect + Echo reacts to you |
| **Mythic** | Loot only | + Screen aura visible to others + custom nameplate + evolves over time |

### Key Principles:
- **Never pay-to-win** — cosmetics only, no gameplay advantage
- **Earn everything by playing** — patience = free access to everything
- **Paying = skipping time** — not buying power
- **Echo models skins naturally** — wears them, comments on them, never pushy
- **Limited editions** — seasonal skins create urgency without FOMO abuse

## 🪩 The Disco Floor: "The Underground"

### What Is It?
Floor 10 — a customizable club space where real nightlife communities create digital venues. The pre-party starts here.

### For Visitors:
- Walk in → hear genre-specific procedural music → see people dancing
- Your avatar auto-dances when on the dance floor (genre-matched moves)
- React with emotes (🔥❤️👏), chat, request vibes
- Browse club directory → find tonight's events → RSVP
- Premium skins glow brighter on the dance floor

### For Clubs/DJs:
- Create your own room (dance floor, DJ booth, bar, lounge, VIP)
- Set genre/BPM/mood → procedural music matches your vibe
- Post tonight's event: lineup, doors, dress code, price
- Your regulars hang out in your room between events
- Analytics: who visited, peak hours, vibe ratings

### Music System (all procedural, no audio files):
- **House**: 4/4 kick, off-beat hats, deep bass, pad chords (120-128 BPM)
- **Techno**: hard kick, rolling hats, acid bass, dark pad (130-140 BPM)
- **Hip-Hop**: boom-bap, 808 bass, vinyl crackle (85-95 BPM)
- **DnB**: breakbeat, reese bass (170-175 BPM)
- **Ambient**: no drums, layered pads, reverb wash (70 BPM)
- **Latin**: clave, congas, piano montuno (100-110 BPM)

### Dance Animations:
- **Per-genre**: house sway, techno nod, hip-hop bounce, DnB step
- **Per-skin**: premium skins get unique dance moves
- **Group sync**: nearby dancers subtly sync timing (social bonding)
- **Dance floor tiles**: color-pulse to BPM, genre-themed palettes

### The Business:
- Free: visit any club, dance, basic avatar
- Premium: skins that glow on dance floor, VIP access
- Club owners: room creation fee OR earn through visitor count
- Featured placement: clubs pay to be on directory front page
- Real-world partnerships: clubs offer IRL perks to loyal digital visitors

## 🧭 Design Principles (Never Break These)

1. **The world IS the interface** — if it needs a tutorial, it's too complex
2. **Free players are first-class citizens** — they're the community, not the product
3. **Every pixel earns its place** — no clutter, no noise, no filler
4. **Real > fake** — real communities, real events, real connections
5. **Fun for one, better with friends** — solo play works, social play shines
6. **Echo is a character, not a feature** — personality drives everything
7. **Quality over quantity** — 5 perfect skins > 50 mediocre ones
8. **Earn everything by playing** — money buys time, not access
9. **Mobile-first, desktop-enhanced** — most people will visit on phones
10. **The disco floor is the front door** — it's what people share, what goes viral
