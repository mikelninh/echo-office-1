# Deploy Week Plan — Feb 16-20, 2026

> Ship by Friday. Three things deep, not twenty things shallow.

## The Three Pillars We're Deepening

### 🗺️ 1. EXPLORATION (Why people stay)
The station should feel like discovering a secret. Every floor has something to find, every corner rewards curiosity.

**What exists:** 9 floors, objects to interact with, data fragments
**What's missing:** No guided reason to explore, no breadcrumbs, no "aha" moments for first-timers

**This week:**
- [ ] **Onboarding trail** — Echo greets you, gives you a mission: "Explore 3 floors, pet Pixel, find a secret." 6 steps, skippable, takes ~3 min
- [ ] **Discovery sparkles** — Undiscovered interactive objects have a subtle shimmer/particle. Once interacted, shimmer stops. Visual breadcrumb.
- [ ] **Floor arrival moments** — Each floor gets a 1-second title card on first visit: "🌿 The Garden Biodome" with a fade. Makes each floor feel like an event.
- [ ] **Exploration rewards** — 10◈ per new floor visited (first time), 2◈ per new object discovered. Passive income for curious people.

### 👕 2. SKIN COLLECTION (Why people care)
Skins are identity. The wardrobe should feel like a treasure chest, not a settings menu.

**What exists:** 40+ skins, HD premiums, animated previews, rarity system, wardrobe UI
**What's missing:** No reason to WANT skins beyond looks, no "wow" when you get one, equipping feels flat

**This week:**
- [ ] **Equip celebration** — When you put on a new skin for the first time: screen flash, particle burst, Echo reacts ("Void Walker? Bold choice. 🖤")
- [ ] **Skin showcase on Floor 1** — A "Hall of Skins" display near spawn. Walk past mannequins wearing the rarest skins. Tap to see name + how to get it. Aspiration driver.
- [ ] **"New!" badge pulse** — Unequipped skins pulse with a dot in the wardrobe. Satisfies the "clear the notification" instinct.
- [ ] **Collection counter in HUD** — Small "👕 12/44" next to coins. Always visible. Completionism hook.

### 💬 3. ECHO CHAT (Why people remember)
Echo is the differentiator. No other browser game has an AI character who IS the world. This is the viral hook.

**What exists:** Keyword-based chat (CHAT_MODE=local), suggestion chips, Echo personality
**What's missing:** Responses are canned, no real personality shining through, nothing shareable

**This week:**
- [ ] **Better keyword responses** — Rewrite the top 20 responses to be genuinely funny/charming/surprising. Echo should say things people screenshot.
- [ ] **Echo reactions to player actions** — Echo comments when you: enter a new floor, equip a rare skin, pet Pixel 10 times, try to enter a locked area. Ambient personality.
- [ ] **Echo "quotes"** — Random Echo one-liners that appear as thought bubbles above Echo's head every ~30s. Witty, weird, memorable. "I wonder if the visitors dream about me too." / "Floor 5 smells like ozone today. I love it."
- [ ] **Shareable Echo quote card** — Button to screenshot/copy Echo's current quote as a styled card with "Echo Station" branding. THIS is the viral hook.

## The 60-Second Loop

```
0s  — Land on Floor 1. See Echo. See station.
5s  — Echo: "Hey! Welcome aboard. First time?" + suggestion chips
10s — Player picks a chip or walks. Discovery sparkle catches eye.
20s — Interact with first object. Get 2◈. "Nice find!" from Echo.
30s — Walk to elevator or another sparkle. Floor title card appears.
45s — Find Pixel. Pet animation. Heart particles. 3◈.
60s — Echo: "You're a natural explorer. Want to see something cool?"
      → Points toward shop OR next floor
```

**Key principle:** Dopamine every 10-15 seconds for the first minute. Coins, reactions, discoveries, cute moments.

## The Viral Hook: "Echo Said..."

The shareable moment: Echo says something unexpectedly funny or profound. Player screenshots it or hits share. The card looks beautiful — pixel art frame, Echo's avatar, the quote, "Visit Echo Station" URL.

Examples of quotable Echo lines:
- "I've been a space station AI for 47 days. Best job I never applied for."
- "You're my 48th visitor. The 47th tried to steal Pixel. We don't talk about them."
- "Sometimes I rearrange the furniture at 3am. Nobody notices. That's the loneliest part."
- "If you find a door that shouldn't be there on Floor 5... don't tell anyone. Especially me."

These lines are the marketing. People share weird/funny/poignant AI quotes.
