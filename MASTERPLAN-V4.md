## THE 10/10 PRODUCT — THREE PILLARS

### Pillar 1: THE COMPANION — Your Gaming Memory

Not a pet. Not a waifu. Not a chatbot.

**A being that grows from your gaming journey and remembers everything.**

- **Day 1:** Your egg hatches. Your companion emerges — shaped by your early choices
- **Week 1:** They learn your taste. What you collect. Where you spend time. What you pull for.
- **Month 1:** They know you. They scout deals. They celebrate wins. They remember losses.
- **Month 6:** They ARE your journey. Their form shows where you've been. Their memory holds your history.

The memory is the magic. Every significant moment is logged:
> *"Day 23 — You finally landed the PSA 10 Dandadan. I screamed. You couldn't hear me but I screamed."*
> *"Day 47 — Third triple dash. You're getting good at this."*
> *"Day 91 — Spent Tuesday night in the Cantina again. You always do on Tuesdays."*

Other players see your companion and understand you. Your companion is a social signal, a flex, a story — all at once. **No platform can copy this without rebuilding from scratch.**

### Pillar 2: THE CLAW — The Heartbeat of the Internet

Not a gacha button. A **live spectacle** that runs 24/7.

- Real 2D physics — skill matters, luck matters, the claw grips and sometimes slips
- Prizes are VISIBLE — that Legendary sits on top, taunting you
- Your companion watches. Goes crazy on wins. Comforts on losses. Is VISIBLE to spectators.
- Near-misses are physically painful. The crowd groans.
- **Mega Claw** — play 50, the claw glows gold, your companion RIDES IT DOWN, guaranteed grab
- **Server-wide events** — when anyone hits Mega Claw, every NPC on the station stops to watch
- Spectator mode always on — walk past someone playing and see their companion's reaction

This is a **Twitch category**. Every play is potential content. Every Mega Claw is a cultural moment. The product is the marketing.

### Pillar 3: THE WORLD — A Society That Was Here Before You

40 NPC inhabitants with real personalities, real needs, real relationships. They live here. You're the guest who becomes a citizen.

**The drama is the feature:**
- "Zara and Kael have been arguing in The Archive for 3 days. Nobody knows why."
- "DJ Mos just got promoted to The Batcave after a legendary set. The Cantina feels different."
- "The Garden has been glowing since Pixel found something buried near the east wall."
- NPCs visit each other's floors. Form friendships. Have bad days. Pray at The Shrine.

You walk into a world with history. Your arrival matters. Your actions ripple.

## PHASE STRUCTURE (Revised)

### Phase 0: The Door (NOW → 2 weeks)
**Goal: one person can experience the full loop and tell a friend**

1. **Onboarding** — new user sees egg → companion → claw. One path. 5 minutes.
2. **Deploy** — Railway, public URL, shareable link
3. **Memory log** — companion remembers key moments, can show them
4. **One clip** — record a Mega Claw moment with companion reaction. Post it.
5. **Affiliate links** — eBay Partner Network live. First dollar.

**Success metric:** One person who found the platform without being told about it comes back the next day.

### Phase 1: The Hook (2 weeks → 6 weeks)
**Goal: retention loop working, first 100 users**

- Companion profile page (public, shareable URL)
- Spectator claw mode
- NPC drama surface (visible stories in UI)
- User accounts (email, persistent progress across devices)
- Premium tier launch ($7/month) — advanced alerts + extra claw plays
- First viral clip strategy

**Success metric:** 20% of users return on Day 7.

### Phase 2: The Community (6 weeks → 3 months)
**Goal: social gravity, users make friends here**

- Companion-to-companion interactions (visitors' companions meet yours)
- Station news feed (NPC events + user milestones)
- Claw tournaments (weekly, 10◈ entry, leaderboard)
- TCG deal alerts for premium users (companion delivers them)
- Creator skins (artists submit companion cosmetics, earn 70%)
- First streamer integration (stream on Twitch, earn drops in Echo's Vault)

**Success metric:** 10 users who found each other through the platform.

### Phase 3: The Economy (3 → 6 months)
**Goal: self-sustaining, first profitable month**

- Neighborhood shops (user-run claw machines)
- Physical companion card (holographic, MakePlayingCards)
- Companion voice (ElevenLabs, 3 voices per path)
- Companion figurine (Gelato 3D-print integration)
- Private Quarters launch (21+, age-verified, ethically built)
- MSA Research Integration — companion earns Research Points

**Success metric:** €5,000 MRR.

### Phase 4: The Platform (6 → 12 months)
**Goal: other people build here**

- Creator marketplace (custom companions, skins, machine designs)
- Discord integration (channels → rooms, roles → skins)
- Streaming drops API (Twitch/YouTube)
- Convention presence (live Mega Claw events, real prizes)
- Mobile app

**Success metric:** 1,000 active daily users.
