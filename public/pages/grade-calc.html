How the math works
The same formula PSA-savvy traders use — now accessible to everyone.
📐
Full EV Formula
Expected Value accounts for all possible grade outcomes — not just PSA 10 or nothing. Each grade has a probability and a sell price. Sum them all up, subtract your costs.
EV = Σ (grade_sell_price × grade_rate) − raw_price − grading_cost
📊
Grade Distribution
We estimate PSA 9, 8, and ≤7 rates from your PSA 10 rate using community averages across TCG grading history. If you have real PSA pop data, enter it for maximum accuracy. PSA 9 typically runs at ~1.8× the PSA 10 rate; PSA 8 at ~1.2×.
⚠️
Pop Dilution Risk
When hundreds of raw copies flood the market, more people grade them → PSA 10 pop grows → price suppression. We apply an EV discount (0–40%) based on raw float size and pop count. High risk = lower expected sell price due to future supply.
