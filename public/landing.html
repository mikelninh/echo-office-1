    04 / PERSONAL MISSION

    Play can point toward something meaningful.

    Multiple System Atrophy is personal to my family. Echo Station explores a long-term idea: can a playful digital world create attention, community and eventually useful participation around rare-disease research?

    The public prototypes do not currently submit research-grade data or claim scientific validation. The ambition is real; the infrastructure and partnerships still need to be earned.

    Honest boundaryThis is currently a product and interaction hypothesis, not an active medical research platform. Any future research contribution would require scientific partners, validated tasks, consent, governance and privacy review.
