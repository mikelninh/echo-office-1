How It Works

1

Add the Bot

Invite Echo Worlds to your Discord server. One click. No config needed.

2

Rooms Auto-Generate

Your channels become rooms instantly. #general → Central Plaza, #music → Listening Lounge, and so on.

3

Members Enter

Type /enter in Discord or click the link. Your world opens in the browser. No download.

4

Walk, Talk, Play

See each other as avatars. Chat syncs both ways. Voice is spatial. Games are real. Your community comes alive.

🧬 The Mission: Cure MSA

Multiple System Atrophy is a rare, devastating neurodegenerative disease with no cure. Echo Worlds dedicates 5% of all platform revenue to MSA research, and every player can contribute directly through citizen science games in The Lab.

Research Points can never be bought — only earned through genuine contribution. Every protein you fold, every scan you classify, adds up.
